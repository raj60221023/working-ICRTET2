About

About the Conference :

It is our great pleasure to welcome Scientists, Engineers, and Researchers from around the world to attend the 1st International Conference on Recents Trends in Engineering and Technology (ICRTET 2024), scheduled for December 20-21, 2024. The primary objective of this conference is to unite academicians and experts from various regions, both domestically and internationally, to share innovations and information. This gathering aims to provide an in-depth analysis of various subjects and update the knowledge of participants from academic, industrial, and research institutions.

Objectives of ICRTET 2024 :

  1. 1. Knowledge Sharing: Create a forum for exchanging information and experiences among Researchers, Academicians, Manufacturers, Consultants, and Authorities in the field of Engineering and Technology.
  2. 2. Networking: Facilitate networking opportunities with experts, speakers, and participants globally.
  3. 3. Collaboration: Encourage professional collaborations between academia and industry for future projects.
  4. 4. Professional Growth: Inspire faculty and researchers by offering new insights and inspiration from industry leaders and academicians.

Conference Features :

  • ● Technical & Panel Discussions: Engage in comprehensive discussions on various topics.
  • ● Industry Interaction: Interact with industry experts to gain practical insights.
  • ● Keynote Speeches: Listen to recognized global experts share the latest developments.
  • ● Paper & Poster Presentations: Present and discuss cutting-edge research findings.
  • ● Special Sessions: Attend focused sessions on selected topics.

Benefits for Participants :

  • ● Networking Opportunities: Connect with global experts, speakers, and fellow participants.
  • ● Professional Collaboration: Explore opportunities for future projects with academia and industry.
  • ● Industry Insights: Gain new perspectives and inspiration from industry leaders.
  • ● Knowledge Update: Stay informed about the latest scientific and technological advancements.

ICRTET 2024 aims to bring together scholars, academicians, scientists, and industrialists to discuss ongoing research in various fields, fostering research relationships between academia and industry.
This conference will address scientific and technological issues presented by esteemed global experts, providing invaluable opportunities for learning and professional development. We look forward to welcoming you to an enriching and inspiring conference experience.

Publication :

Extended versions of papers will be submitted for publication in Scopus Indexed conference proceedings on additional charges.
All other accepted papers presented in the conference will be published in ICRTET conference proceedings.

Call for Papers Page

Scope of the Conference :

The 1st International Conference on Engineering and Technology (ICRTET 2024) aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers, and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results in all aspects of Engineering and Technology. This premier interdisciplinary forum offers researchers, engineers, and practitioners the opportunity to present their latest research results, ideas, developments, and applications.

Conference Highlights:

  • ● Collaboration Opportunities: Engage with national and international organizations of repute for collaborative ventures.
  • ● Extensive Participation: ICRTET 2024 anticipates a large number of participants and submissions from around the world.
  • ● Interdisciplinary Forum: Present and discuss the latest advancements across various fields of Engineering and Technology.

Topics of Interest:

ICRTET 2024 invites submissions on a broad range of topics, including but not limited to



Digital Forensics, Signal and Image Processing, Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI), Power Electronics, Manufacturing Engineering, Solid Mechanics, Fluid Mechanics, Concrete Science and Technology, Construction Planning, Scheduling, and Control, Explainable AI, Automation Systems, Computer Vision, Digital Twins, Mobile Systems, Cyber Physical Machines, High-Performance Computing, Agile Systems


By bringing together experts from diverse fields, ICRTET 2024 seeks to foster an environment of collaboration and innovation, addressing the latest challenges and advancements in Engineering and Technology. We look forward to your participation in ICRTET 2024, where you can share your research, engage with global experts, and contribute to the ongoing dialogue in Engineering and Technology.
